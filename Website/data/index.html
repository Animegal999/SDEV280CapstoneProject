Project Overview

The purpose of the project is to find out a general idea of what everyone's Ecological Footprint is. Since we cannot possibly interview 8 billion different people at once, we chose a smaller sample size to represent a population, which was 120 people. We hoped to achieve a general idea of a country's overall Ecological Footprint and Number of Earths that a person from said country might use in their everyday life. With the measured results, we hope to take the next steps in reducing our Ecological Footprints.
